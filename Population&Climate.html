Culture & Seasons

Mpumalanga Province is located east of Gauteng, south of Limpopo, north of KwaZulu-Natal, and west of Mozambique. The main cultural groups associated with this province are Swati, Ndebele, Tsonga, Pedi, and Zulu-speaking people.

This province has interesting seasonal patterns, with hot wet summers and dry cold winters. Mpumalanga experiences summer, winter, spring, and autumn seasons.
